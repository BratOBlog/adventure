Sword and sorcery adventure generator

What this generator is about

This is a sword and sorcery adventure generator, where you can generate a fun campain or a writing prompt.
Based on the Random Sword and Sorcery Adventure generator of Ben Ball.
Some aspects are altered to get a more general feel, so writers could also enjoy the generator.
Currently you can generate each part individually to your liking or get the whole story by clicking a button.

"Sword and sorcery (S&S) is a subgenre of fantasy characterized by sword-wielding heroes engaged in exciting and violent adventures.
Elements of romance, magic, and the supernatural are also often present.
Unlike works of high fantasy, the tales, though dramatic, focus on personal battles rather than world-endangering matters.
Sword and sorcery commonly overlaps with heroic fantasy."
Sword and Sorcery page from Wikipedia
