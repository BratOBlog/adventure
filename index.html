What this generator is about

This is a sword and sorcery adventure generator, where you can generate a fun campain or a writing prompt. Based on the Random Sword and Sorcery Adventure generator of Ben Ball. Some aspects are altered to get a more general feel, so writers could also enjoy the generator. Currently you can generate each part individually to your liking. I'm working on method where you can get the whole story by clicking a button.

Let's begin! All you need is to click the buttons. By refreshing the page it will go to idle state.
